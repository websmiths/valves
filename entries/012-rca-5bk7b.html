RCA 5BK7B

Source: boxes-2.jpg

The 5BK7B (and its close relatives 5BK7 and 5BK7A) is a miniature medium-mu twin triode with a 4.7 V heater designed for use as a combined oscillator and mixer (frequency converter) stage in television receivers. The 'B' suffix denotes a later revision with tightened electrical specifications.

Typical applications

The 5BK7B was used principally in the front-end tuner sections of television receivers as a local-oscillator / mixer (frequency converter) tube. Its 4.7 V heater fitted series-string heater chains common in transformerless TV sets of the era. The two triode sections could be used as a self-oscillating mixer, simplifying the tuner circuit.
