Radiotron 6X5GT

Entry #006 · Source: boxes-1.jpeg

Indirectly-heated full-wave rectifier on the octal base — the small-signal partner to the 5Y3GT for receiver-sized B+ supplies. Heater is 6.3 V so it can share a transformer winding with the signal valves, simplifying AC/DC and car-radio designs.

Typical applications

The 6X5GT was the rectifier of choice for valve car radios and small AC mains receivers from the late 1930s onward, because the 6.3 V heater meant one transformer winding (or vibrator inverter) could supply every valve in the set. Also common in test gear, small organs and intercom amplifiers.
